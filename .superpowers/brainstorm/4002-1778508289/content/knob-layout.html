Hero graphic redesign — which layout?

4 steps: Audit · Model · Tune · Pilot-ready. Knob rotates between them. Two layout approaches:

A — Clock-face (N/E/S/W)

4 steps at 12/3/6/9 o'clock positions. Knob in center with a pointer line that rotates to each active step. Clean, symmetrical.

B — Arc dial (270° sweep)

Steps on a 270° arc like a volume knob. Knob center shows current step name. Gap at bottom. More instrument-like feel.
